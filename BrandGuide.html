Logo Dos & Don'ts

  • Use icons on dark/gradient backgrounds Ensures clarity
  • Keep consistent padding and sizing
  • High contrast for readability
  • Don’t stretch/distort the logo
  • Don’t place on noisy images
  • Don’t rotate off baseline

Templates & Social

  • Business Cards: Logo, contact, gradient strip Keep icons left-aligned
  • Email Signature: Icon, name/title, contact links
  • Slide Decks: Gradient title, clean headers/footers
  • Social: Icon avatar, gradient covers, branded CTAs
Use overlays, coral accents, and geometric patterns for brand consistency.

Iconography & Patterns

  • Minimal, line-based icons
  • Subtle hexagon/circuit patterns in headers
  • Use dark opacity for texture layering (8–15%)
  • Always maintain clarity and contrast
Geometric, grid, and floating line elements allowed for supporting graphics.
